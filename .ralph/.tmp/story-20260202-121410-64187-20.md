### US-020: Add module-level logger to whatsapp_sender.py
Status: in_progress
Depends on: US-015

Description:
As a developer, I want whatsapp_sender.py to have a module-level logger with structured logging for send failures.

Acceptance Criteria:

- [ ] Example: logger.error('WAHA send failed after retries', extra={'max_retries': max_retries})
- [ ] Negative case: No phone numbers or message text in log messages
